1.3 Personal Information: Refers to the information that can be used independently, or jointly with other information, to identify or contact a specific user, such as the user name, date of birth, ID card number, address, phone number, account and password (“Personal Information”). 2.1 What kind of Personal Information will we collect

A. When you create a 360 account, download or update the 360 OS Software, or participate in the 360 OS online survey or other interactions with us, we will ask you to provide your Personal Information, including but not limited to the following: name, display picture, status and personal messages, contact list, telephone number, email address, language preference, 360 ID or other account credentials, settings, service provider information and IP address, country and time zone, information about the use of the 360 OS Software’s functionality and the software and hardware utilized in conjunction with the 360 OS Software, including device or computer information (for example, device PIN, IMEI, IMSI, UDID, MAC address or similar identifiers, and device model) or other information permitted by law to collect. In some cases, address book information, device location data (including real-time location information), calendar and reminder entries, and photos may be accessed by the 360 OS Software and Processed by us to provide functionality that uses this information as part of or in conjunction with the 360 OS Software.

B. When you enable location-based program services and functions provided by 360 OS Software, your current or saved location will be sample periodically. For example, when you perform location-based search, use location-based advertisement, and use location to optimize weather function or ask for map information, your location information may be sent to the 360 OS so that we can provide you with more relevant and correct content. Please review the settings menu of the applicable device or application software to determine how you may be able to turn off or adjust location features.

C. When you use the cloud platform of 360 OS, which may include remote access, storage or back-up functionality, to back up your important data such as contacts, short messages, call records and pictures, you represent and warrant that you have all necessary consents to provide such data to us and agree such data may be processed by 360 OS.

D. When you enter, provide to, or integrate with the 360 OS Software to share your content or send information, we will collect the Personal Information provided by you about the recipients of your content or information, including but not limited to the name, Email address and phone number.

E. When you use the upgrade service of the 360 OS, we may collect information such as your device name, device identification information (IMEI, ESN, MEID, and SN), system and application versions, operating system and browser versions, region and language settings, operator’s network code, operator’s network ID (PLMN), application use status, and IP address used to access the Internet. We may collect the aforementioned information for statistics and analysis purposes.

F. When the 360 OS Software encounters an exception and you choose to send related information to us so that we or our partner can analyze the failure, we may collect related information such as the device model, software name and version, device identification code, error log.

G. When we provide some services that allow you to synchronize, share and store data, we will collect and store the data selected by you to upload, download or implement such functions.

C. You are not required to provide the Personal Information that we have requested, but if you choose not to do so, in most cases we will not be able to provide you with our products or services or respond to any queries you may have.

D. We collect your Personal Information to improve our products and services and helps us to provide you with personalized service, so that we can better meet your requirements, customize services for you, and provide you with better product or service experience.

E. We may also use your Personal Information to send important notices, such as communications about purchases and changes to our terms, conditions and policies. Since such information is important to your interaction with us, you may not opt out of receiving such communications.

F. We collect your Personal Information to meet legal and regulatory requirements and to respond to emergency situations, such as to respond to court orders, warrants or legal processes and to provide emergency assistance in situations that may threaten the life or physical safety of you or others.

G. For any other purposes that you have consented and permitted or required by any applicable law.

We will also collect Non-Personal Information. We may collect, use, transfer and disclose Non-Personal Information for the purpose to enhance functions and improve user experience.

A. Certain types of Non-Personal Information and the circumstances that we may collect and use such information are set out below:

a) When you use Google or Facebook account, download and update the 360 OS Software, participate in the 360 OS online survey or have other interactions with us, we will collect your information such as the occupation, language, postal code, area code, and time zone when you use the 360 OS Software.

b) When you join the 360 OS “User Experience Improvement Program”, we will collect your use of various 360 OS Software functions in the anonymous mode as needed, so as to analyze your habits of using the 360 OS Software and its services. Furthermore, when the system breaks down or fails, we will collect the environmental information of your device, which will help us diagnose the error.

c) When you use the 360 OS theme, voice assistant, browser and other functions, we will collect the information related to your use behavior recorded at your server, such as uploading, downloading, synchronizing and browsing.

d) 360 OS online service, interactive application software, Email messages and advertising may use cookies and other technologies like pixel label and website beacon to collect and store your Non-Personal Information.

f) We will also collect information related to your use of 360 OS Software and its services or other activities. We will summarize this kind of information to help us provide users with more useful information and learn which parts of 360 OS Software and its services the customers are most interested in.

B. If we combine the Non-Personal Information with Personal Information, this kind of information will be deemed Personal Information when they are combined for storage.

The operator undertakes to analyze the information only for the purpose of improving the quality of service or providing a customized service or technology and will not disclose the information to the outside world in any form that can identify your personally.
